# Status

- General proof: supplied in `report/theoreme_asymptotique.md`.
- Finite verification: deterministic, dependency-free, two-prime modular
  checks; not used as a substitute for the proof.
- Positive range: all `m>=3`, `n>=m`, `2n<m^2`.
- Sharp failure range for this invariant: all `2n>=m^2`.
- Literature: the quadratic border lower bound and Hessian-divisibility
  framework are known; no novelty claim.
- Not proved: any super-quadratic lower bound, any super-polynomial lower bound,
  or `VP != VNP`.
